Welcome to FCAN Morang

The Federation of Contractors' Associations of Nepal (FCAN), Morang District Chapter is the official representative body of construction contractors in Morang district. Established in 1995, we have been serving the construction industry by advocating for contractors' rights, providing professional development, and ensuring quality construction standards in the region.
