Committee Functions & Responsibilities

Policy Making

Formulate policies and strategies for the development of construction sector in Morang district.

Member Representation

Represent members' interests in discussions with government and other stakeholders.

Event Organization

Organize meetings, workshops, training programs, and other events for members.

Dispute Resolution

Mediate and resolve disputes among members or between members and clients.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Monitor construction projects and evaluate member performance and compliance.

Capacity Building

Arrange training and skill development programs for member contractors.
